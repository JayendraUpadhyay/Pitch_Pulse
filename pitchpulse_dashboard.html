Workload & Fatigue Risk Index

Every appearance is time-stamped and matched against the player's rest gap before it. Back-to-back matches (≤1 day rest) are weighted 3× heavier than a normal turnaround, 2-day gaps 1.5×, tapering to zero past 4 days — producing a strain-per-match score that a fitness staff could use to flag rotation candidates before an injury, not after one.

Highest Strain-Per-Match min. 15 matches
Risk Score Distribution
Risk score = min-max normalized strain-per-match across all qualifying players (0–100 scale). This flags density of short-rest turnarounds, not career length — a player with 200 relaxed matches scores lower than one with 40 congested ones.
02

Clutch Performance Index

Raw Player-of-the-Match counts reward volume. Our clutch-weighted score triples the value of any POTM earned in an eliminator / knockout stage, surfacing players who perform when the stakes — not just the scoreboard — are highest.

Clutch-Weighted Leaderboard
POTM Rate vs Sample Size
Clutch score = (league-stage POTM × 1) + (knockout POTM × 3). Rate% is POTM awards divided by total matches played, controlling for players with fewer appearances.
